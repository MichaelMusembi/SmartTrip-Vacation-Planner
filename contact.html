Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a trip through SmartTrip?

You can book directly through our website by selecting your destination, choosing available packages, and completing the secure checkout process. Alternatively, you can contact our travel advisors for personalized assistance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, M-Pesa, and bank transfers. All payments are processed through secure, encrypted channels.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our standard policy allows free cancellation up to 30 days before your trip. Cancellations between 15-30 days receive a 50% refund, while those within 14 days are non-refundable. Some premium packages may have different terms.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance packages that cover medical emergencies, trip cancellations, lost luggage, and more. We strongly recommend purchasing insurance for all international travel.
